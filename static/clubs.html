In the past few months, a few clubs have sprung up in IISER Kolkata. While we are still warming up and setting up the clubs, here is a list of some clubs that you might want to join. Some clubs also have a Google groups to which you can subscribe.

Literary

Writing, extempore, debates, quizzes. We produce the annual and wall magazines as well as the newsletter which contains the details of happening stuff on the campus. Find us at the iiserkol-litclub group.

Astronomy and Model making

Gaze at the night skies and catch a glimpse of stars through a telescope, or tinker with electronics and make gadgets. Discussions at the astroiiserk list. Latest news and other stuff on astro.iiserk.net.

Sports

There are several sports clubs, one for each sport, ranging from cricket and football to table-tennis. We have regular tournaments in all of these.

Music

Guitars, drums and other things to keep you musically occupied.

Movies

A movie every weekend on Friday evenings. Look out for the posters in the canteen!

Arts

Design stuff. Have fun.

Nature

Watch the birds, plant trees and study Mother Nature.
